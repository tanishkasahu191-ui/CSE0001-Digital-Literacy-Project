# Social Media Checklist for Responsible Digital Use

| Category | Do's  | Don'ts  |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Privacy** | Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all accounts. | Don't share your live location or travel plans in real-time. |
| **Identity** | Use a professional profile picture for LinkedIn and GitHub. | Don't share sensitive data like Aadhaar, OTPs, or Reg No. |
| **Content** | Fact-check news from official sources before resharing. | Don't post content that uses offensive or unprofessional language. |
| **Safety** | Keep your personal life and public posts separate. | Don't click on "Win a Prize" links or suspicious advertisements. |
| **Etiquette** | Ask for permission before tagging friends in your photos. | Don't engage with or feed online trolls and cyberbullies. |
