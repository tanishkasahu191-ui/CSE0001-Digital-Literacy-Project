
## Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

For this task, I acted as a Student Digital Ambassador to design a one-page infographic using Canva. The design focuses on three pillars: defining digital literacy, practicing safe internet habits, and maintaining a professional online presence. I chose a modern, colorful layout with clear icons to ensure the information is accessible and engaging for my batchmates.
## One thing I found particularly interesting was the challenge of 'visual hierarchy'—deciding which information was most critical to highlight first. I realized that while technical skills are important, safety (like UPI security) is the most immediate concern for students. The most difficult part was condensing broad topics like 'cybersecurity' into three simple, actionable bullet points. This process taught me that effective digital communication requires a balance between being thorough and being concise.
